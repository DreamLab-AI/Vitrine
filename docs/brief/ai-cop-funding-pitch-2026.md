## Slide 4
### 3. The external partner challenge being solved
- DreamLab AI has an establish reputation in agential AI development in virtual spaces. However in order to increase its internal capabilities and grow its offer, it needs to develop a workflow, and training, that bridges the virtual and the physical worlds efficiently.
- This tooling around physical and digital telepresence, in virtual and mixed reality spaces, has cross sectoral appeal - from out-of-home-entertainment, to archiving, collections management, and commissioning.

## Slide 5

## Slide 6
### 5. The impact (outputs and outcomes) expected from the project and longer-term plans
- The end point will be a business case proposition and proof of principle toolset for use as a training offer in agential tools as “building factory” that fills the Capability Gap in any sector that requires preservation, archiving, and emulation of complex physical spaces and time-based immersive experiences.
- Explore wider implications with international bodies including ARA – The Archive and Records Association and ICA, the International Council on Archives, Rhizome (NYC), and Onasis ONX (NYC and Greece) towards an EU Commission bid (Horizon deadline 2027)
- The project is designed to enable cross-disciplinary CPD and KE for internal staff, including technical staff, with direct input from AI experts outside of the University.
